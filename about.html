About Us

Passion. Tradition. Authentic Flavors. Taste the heart of Nepalese and Indian cuisine today!

Discover the authentic flavors of Nepalese, Indian, and Indo-Chinese cuisine in a warm and inviting atmosphere. Our menu features traditional dishes with a modern twist, crafted with the finest ingredients.

Inspired by the rich culinary traditions of Nepal and India, we bring family recipes and authentic flavors to every table. Don’t miss our steaming momos, buttery naan, and flavorful Indo-Chinese stir-fries.

Whether you’re here for a casual lunch, a night out with friends, or a special celebration, our cozy yet lively ambiance makes every meal memorable. Our mission is to bring people together over authentic flavors, great drinks, and a relaxed, welcoming atmosphere. Come taste our delicious dishes, enjoy refreshing drinks, and experience service that feels like home!
