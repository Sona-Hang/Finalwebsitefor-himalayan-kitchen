House Crafted Cocktails

Gurkha's Sip A bold yet smooth drink made with dark rum, honey, and citrus. A light touch of spice gives it warmth and depth. Inspired by Nepal’s Gurkha warriors, this cocktail is strong, balanced, and made for those who enjoy classic flavors with character. $15.00
Kanchanjunga Cooler Fresh and clean, this cocktail is inspired by Nepal’s mountain breeze. Vodka is mixed with citrus and ginger for a bright, refreshing taste. A hint of maple syrup adds gentle sweetness. Light and easy to sip, it’s great for warm days or relaxed evenings. $15.00
Bow to Everest This bold drink brings together tequila, ginger, and blue curaçao for a bright, citrusy taste. It’s cool and smooth with a hint of spice. A refreshing cocktail that stands tall — just like Everest. Made for those who enjoy strong and fresh flavors. $17.00
Sherpa Silk Rich and creamy, this cocktail blends espresso, ice cream, and spirits. It’s cold, smooth, and slightly sweet with a caffeine kick. A perfect drink for the evening — bold and comforting, just like the spirit of the Sherpa people. $16.00
Thamel Nights A lively and colorful mix of cranberry, peach, and citrus. Light, fruity, and full of energy, with a subtle shimmer that reflects the vibrant nightlife of Thamel. Fun, stylish, and made for those who love a little sparkle with their evening. $15.00
Petals of Pokhara A floral and soft drink with lavender, coconut, and fresh lime. Lightly sweet and very refreshing. Inspired by Pokhara’s peaceful lakes and gardens, this cocktail is gentle, calming, and full of natural charm. $16.00
Tequila Tundra Cool and crisp with a bold heart. This tequila cocktail mixes blueberry and lime for a fresh, fruity taste. It’s both icy and bright, with a strong finish underneath. A perfect balance of chill and strength in every sip. $17.00
